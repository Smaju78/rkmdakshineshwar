Serve in the Spirit of Shiva Jnane Jiva Seva

See God in every being you serve - at the Math in person, or from anywhere online.

"They alone live, who live for others."
- SWAMI VIVEKANANDA

Ramakrishna Math, Dakshineshwar welcomes volunteers who wish to serve in the spirit of Shiva Jnane Jiva Seva - seeing God in every being they serve. Whether you can offer your time in person or contribute your skills remotely, there is a place for you in our mission.

At the Math - Physical Seva

🙏 Daily Seva

Assist in daily worship preparations, temple cleaning, flower arrangements, and maintenance of the shrine and campus.

🍚 Kitchen & Prasad Service

Help with cooking, serving, and distribution of prasad during regular days and special festival celebrations.

🎉 Event Support

Assist during holy day celebrations, devotee conventions, retreats, and special programmes - logistics, crowd management, and guest coordination.

🤝 Relief & Outreach

Participate in distribution drives - food, clothing, study materials for underprivileged families, widow relief, and medical camp assistance.

🌿 Campus & Garden

Help maintain the ashrama garden, campus beautification, and upkeep of the Mayer Naba Nahabat retreat space.

From Anywhere - Online Volunteering

💻 Digital & Social Media

Help manage the Math's social media presence, create content, edit photos/videos from events, and maintain the website.

📖 Teaching & Mentoring

Conduct online classes for students at the Swami Vivekananda Computer Centre, tutor underprivileged children, or assist with youth programme content.

🎨 Design & Creative

Design posters, invitations, and event materials for celebrations, programmes, and outreach campaigns.

📝 Writing & Translation

Help translate spiritual content, write articles for publications, or prepare educational material in Bengali, Hindi, or English.

Get in Touch

There is no formal application process. Simply reach out to us and let us know how you would like to serve. We welcome all - regardless of age, background, or experience.
