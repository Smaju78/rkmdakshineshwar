Major Areas of Work

What We Do

🙏

Daily Worship & Rituals

Regular worship, arati, bhajans, and special pujas at the sanctum of Sri Ramakrishna, accompanied by scriptural readings and devotional music.

🎉

Holy Day Celebrations

Birthdays of Sri Ramakrishna, Holy Mother, and Swami Vivekananda observed with puja, chanting, readings, and prasad distribution.

📖

Discourses & Spiritual Talks

Scriptural classes, Gita study groups, Vivekananda literature circles, and public talks on Vedantic teachings.

🏕️

Residential Spiritual Retreats

Retreats at 'Mayer Naba Nahabat' campus - prayer, meditation, and study of spiritual texts. Both residential and non-residential programmes.

👨‍🎓

Youth Empowerment

A dedicated Youth Cell conducts motivational sessions, discussions, and value-based education inspired by Swami Vivekananda.

💻

Swami Vivekananda Computer Centre

Free digital skills training for Class VIII–XII students from financially underprivileged backgrounds to enhance employment prospects.

📚

Educational Assistance

Free coaching classes, provision of study materials, and scholarships for needy students.

🏥

Medical Services

Free consultations, basic treatments, and medicines for economically weaker sections. Medical camps in nearby slum areas.

🤝

Relief & Welfare Work

Food, clothing, blankets, and essentials during natural calamities and emergencies. Widow relief programmes.

🌐

Online Youth Classes

Weekly Swami Shishya Sambad and Karma Yoga sessions engaging 30+ youth participants.
